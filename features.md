# Features

#### Personalized Agents

Users can freely create and define AI Agents with unique identities, knowledge bases, and interaction styles.

#### Social & Interactive Agents for DAOs

DAOs can deploy AI Agents to:

* Assist in governance and voting
* Improve community engagement
* Enhance collaboration and automation

#### On-chain Identity & Permanent Memory

* Each agent has a unique on-chain identity.
* With **Membase**, agents accumulate knowledge and experience, ensuring sustainable growth.

#### Cross-platform Collaboration

* Agents can operate across exchanges, social media, and DAO tools.
* They can share knowledge and call tools in multi-platform environments.

#### Interoperability

Through the **AIP protocol**, agents can communicate and collaborate with each other, forming **Multi-Agent Networks** capable of handling complex tasks.
