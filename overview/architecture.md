# Architecture

### Features

* **Personalized Agents**\
  Users can freely create and define AI Agents with personalized characteristics, granting them unique identities, knowledge, and interaction styles.
* **DAO-focused Social & Interactive Agents**\
  Supports decentralized organizations (DAOs) in deploying and using AI Agents to enhance governance, collaboration, and communication.
* **On-chain Identity & Permanent Memory**\
  Each agent, powered by **Membase**, possesses a unique on-chain identity and long-term memory, enabling continuous knowledge and experience accumulation.
* **Cross-platform Collaboration**\
  Agents can operate across exchanges, social media, and DAO tools, enabling knowledge sharing and tool integration.
* **Interoperability (via AIP Protocol)**\
  Agents can not only interact with each other but also collaborate to complete complex tasks, forming a **multi-agent collaboration network**.
